Corporate Buying Guide

For regulated industries (banking, healthcare, government):

  • TPM 2.0 with hardware encryption
  • Biometric authentication (fingerprint + IR camera)
  • Self-healing BIOS
  • SmartCard reader for CAC/PIV cards
  • vPro for remote management

  • Contact vendor enterprise sales for 10-25% discounts on 10+ units
  • Standardize on 2-3 models for easier IT management
  • Negotiate 4-5 year extended warranties
  • Request device pre-imaging with your corporate OS build

Executive Purchase Tip

When evaluating premium business laptops, calculate Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) over 4 years including: purchase price, warranty extensions, docking stations, and estimated resale value. Many "expensive" laptops actually cost less long-term due to higher durability and resale value.
