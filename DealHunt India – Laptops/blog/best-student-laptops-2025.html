Best Student Laptops 2025

Affordable, durable, and powerful enough for classes, coding, and Netflix

Updated: July 2025 Under ₹50,000

As a student in 2025, you need a laptop that can handle online classes, research, coding assignments, and entertainment without breaking your budget. After testing 22 models, we've selected these 7 best laptops that offer the perfect balance of performance, battery life, and durability.

Student Discount Tip: Always check your university's tech portal for special education pricing (typically 5-15% off) before purchasing!

Student Laptop Requirements

Battery Life

Minimum 8 hours for all-day classes. Look for 45Wh+ batteries with power-efficient processors.

Performance

8GB RAM minimum, SSD storage, and at least an i3/Ryzen 3 processor for multitasking.

Durability

Spill-resistant keyboards and MIL-STD durability help survive dorm life.

Portability

Under 1.8kg weight and compact size for easy carrying between classes.

Must-Have Student Features

Webcam Quality

With hybrid classes still common, look for:

  • Minimum 720p (1080p preferred)
  • Privacy shutter
  • Noise-canceling mics
Connectivity

Essential ports for students:

  • 2+ USB-A ports for flash drives
  • HDMI for projectors
  • SD card slot for media students
For Engineering Students

If you're studying CS/Engineering, prioritize these specs:

  • 16GB RAM: For running IDEs and VMs smoothly
  • Ryzen 5/i5: Better multi-core performance for coding
  • Linux compatibility: Check for driver support

Consider stretching budget to ₹55k for Dell Inspiron 14 with 16GB RAM if possible.
