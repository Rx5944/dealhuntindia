Best Laptops Under ₹30,000 (2025)

Real-world tested budget laptops that don't compromise on essential performance

Updated: July 2025 SSD Mandatory No HDD Picks

The sub-₹30k laptop market has dramatically improved in 2025, with SSDs becoming standard and better processors entering this price bracket. After testing 15 models, we present the 5 best options that deliver genuine value without the typical budget laptop compromises.

Warning: Avoid any laptop in this range with an HDD instead of SSD - the performance difference is night and day for basic tasks.

What to Expect at ₹30,000?

Realistic Positives
  • SSD boot drives (256GB minimum)
  • Windows 11 ready (with some bloatware)
  • Decent 1080p video playback
  • 8-10 Chrome tabs with basic extensions
Common Limitations
  • Plastic build quality
  • 720p webcams (at best)
  • 4-6 hour battery life
  • No backlit keyboards

Budget Buying Tips

Do's
  • Prioritize SSD over HDD
  • Look for 8GB RAM (not 4GB)
  • Check upgrade options
  • Consider refurbished premium models
Don'ts
  • Avoid "Pentium" processors
  • Skip 1366×768 displays
  • Don't expect gaming
  • Avoid unknown brands
